The Code

Seven principles. One structure.

1

Community

Nothing is built alone.

Community means shared responsibility, shared language, shared protection.

2

Clarity

Clarity before action.

Families must understand who they are, what they are building, and why.

3

Consistency

Wealth is disciplined repetition.

Consistency compounds. Inconsistency resets.

4

Competence

Inheritance without competence collapses.

Children must be trained. Adults must refine.

5

Coordination

Individual success is fragile.

Coordinated success is durable.

6

Contribution

Wealth without contribution corrupts.

Every member must add value.

7

Continuity

Can it outlive you?

This is the ultimate goal.
